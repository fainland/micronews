Minnesota shooting suspect and wife were ‘preppers,’ FBI affidavit says - The Washington Post

Vance Boelter, charged with the fatal shooting of a Minnesota lawmaker and her husband, allegedly instructed his wife to prepare for the aftermath, indicating they were 'preppers,' according to an FBI affidavit.
